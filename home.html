Myanmar

Myanmar is a unique country located in Southeast Asia. In fact, Myanmar is one of the most popular destinations in Asia. It has something for everyone – backpackers, couples, solo travelers, luxury travelers, etc. Myanmar offers several experiences – from street food to trekking, from traditional ceremonies to outdoor activities, from busy markets to remote villages, from amazing temples to beautiful views – you should find something that suits your needs and interests.

Food

When it comes to food, there's a variety of restaurants available. Local cuisine can be enjoyed for less than MMK 5000, while upscale dining options are also available if you prefer to splurge. The most affordable choices are typically found in local eateries and night markets.

Activities

Some activities such as hiking or beach bumming are free, offering budget-friendly options for travelers. However, when it comes to exploring the intricate temples and pagodas, embarking on scenic boat rides across Inle Lake, embarking on memorable trekking adventures, or soaring high in a hot-air balloon ride to witness breathtaking vistas, you will indeed find these experiences to be worth the cost, and it's important to note that for these unique adventures, you will have to pay for it."You can browse activities in Myanmar here to have an idea of the costs.

Transportation

Getting around Myanmar is quite straightforward. You can fly, take a bus, or use the train. For bookings, you have two options: use 12GO or visit a local travel agency.

Safety In Myanmar

Overall, Myanmar is a safe travel destination.Of course, you should always lock your valuables especially if you’re staying in a hostel.

Use common sense, and you'll navigate the country safely. Locals are friendly and supportive if you need assistance. Be aware that certain areas are affected by civil conflict, and tourists are advised against visiting. While there are ongoing issues, they typically don't impact tourists in popular areas.
